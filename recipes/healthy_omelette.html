My Healthy Omelette

Asparagus Omelette Description:

This asparagus omelette is a healthy and tasty snack, perfect for breakfast & getting your day started on the right foot.

This recipe is easy to follow, doesnt require many ingredients, and tastes Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 100g Asparagus
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • 3x large eggs
  • Parsely
  • Cashew nuts
  • Tbsp water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Roast cashews in a pan until golden brown (this releases the flavour)
  2. Combine the following in a bowl:
    • Eggs
    • Parsely
    • Water
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Cheese
    • The previously roasted cashews
  3. Mix Bowl
  4. Add butter and olive oil into the pan you roasted the cashews in
  5. Add the omelette mix to the pan
  6. Add the asparagus to the pan, once the omelette has begun to set (but is still fluid)
