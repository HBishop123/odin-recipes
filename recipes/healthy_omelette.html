My Healthy Omelette

Asparagus Omelette Description:

This asparagus omelette is a healthy and tasty snack, perfect for breakfast & getting your day started on the right foot.

This recipe is easy to follow, doesnt require many ingredients, and tastes Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 100g Asparagus
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • 3x large eggs
  • Parsely
  • Cashew nuts
  • Tbsp water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
