Simple Pasta Dish

Simple Pasta Description:

This simple pasta dish is a very simple yet tasty dish, perfect for when you're on a budgey

This recipe is easy to follow, doesnt require many ingredients, and tastes Magnificent!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta until ready
  2. Strain Pasta
  3. Pour Pasta into a bowl
  4. Add Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper
  5. MIX!
  6. Enjoy.
